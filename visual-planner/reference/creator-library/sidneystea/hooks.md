1. Bold urgency text center-screen (ALL CAPS, emojis)
2. Celebrity/authority/news image overlaid upper-right

## Hook Formula

```
[URGENCY VERB] + [SUBJECT] + [SHOCKING CLAIM] + [EMOJI]
```

Examples:
- "They tried to HIDE this from us"
- "RFK Jr. is making doctors LOSE their jobs"
- "I CANT BELIEVE SHE LET THIS SLIP"
- "This is getting DARK"
- "we are gonna regret this fr fr"
- "this is not a joke anymore"

## Text Style

- ALL CAPS on key words (HIDE, LOSE, DARK, SLIP)
- Emojis: 🤯 ‼️ 😱 ⚠️ 🤭
- Secondary text creates a reason to stay: "Listen for your safety" / "Why is nobody talking about this"
- White bold text, no yellow highlight (different from wellnessmaddie1)

## Key Finding

The hook power comes from the OUTRAGE TEXT, not the visual. The greenscreen image is just a trust/context signal. The speaker's face provides urgency through expression alone. This style is about hook copywriting, not visual production.
