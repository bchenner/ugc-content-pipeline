# Mirror Selfie Prehook

Real or AI-generated mirror selfie showing the person's body condition. Reads as authentic UGC — "this is me, this is my problem." The most relatable prehook type.

## What's On Screen

- Person standing in front of bathroom/bedroom mirror, phone visible in reflection
- Body condition clearly visible: bloating, skin texture, weight, cellulite, hair loss
- Messy/lived-in background (unmade bed, clothes on floor, cardboard boxes) adds authenticity
- No text overlay needed — the image speaks for itself

## Examples Observed

- burner_001 (0:00-0:03): Woman in white tank top and grey shorts, mirror selfie in messy bedroom, pointing at thigh skin condition. Phone (iPhone) visible in hand

## Visual Details

- Vertical (9:16) mirror selfie framing
- Phone visible in the reflection (signals "real person took this")
- Background clutter is GOOD — messy rooms, real bathrooms, laundry piles. This is anti-polished
- Lighting: bathroom fluorescent, bedroom natural, unflattering. NOT ring light
- Person may be pointing at or touching the problem area
- Duration: 2-5s. Quick establish-and-cut
- Hard cut to main speaking format after

## Pipeline

- Image Prompter: photorealistic mirror selfie. Include phone in hand, mirror reflection, messy background
- Key prompt elements: "mirror selfie", "phone visible in reflection", narrative clutter (specific mess items)
- No Veo needed (static image, 2-3s in timeline)
- This is one of the hardest types to get right with AI — mirrors, reflections, and phones create consistency challenges. May need multiple generations
