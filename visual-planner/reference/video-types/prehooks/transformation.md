# Transformation Before/After Prehook

Sequential or side-by-side body shots showing a visible transformation. The most common prehook type across all video formats.

## What's On Screen

- Before state: body part showing the problem (thinning hair, skin condition, weight, dull skin)
- After state: same body part showing improvement (thicker hair, clear skin, weight loss, glow)
- May be two separate clips (before → flash → after) or a single split-screen

## Examples Observed

- burner_002: Doctor standing next to patient, "day 40" text overlay — weight loss transformation
- podcast_dailyhealthtalk: Silent before/after skin clips → hard cut to podcast desk

## Visual Details

- No speaker visible (or speaker as tiny PiP in corner)
- Text overlay: "day 1" / "day 40" or similar time markers
- Before state is often dimmer/warmer, after state is brighter/cooler
- Duration: 3-8s total (1.5-4s per state)
- Hard cut or flash transition between before and after

## Pipeline

- Image Prompter: 2 images (before state + after state) or 1 split-screen composite
- No Veo needed (static images or simple zoom/pan in post)
- Text overlays added in post-production
