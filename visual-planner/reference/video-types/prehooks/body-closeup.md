# Body Close-Up Prehook

Macro close-up of the symptom or body condition. Visceral, attention-grabbing, sometimes gross. The close-up creates immediate relatability — "that looks like MY problem."

## What's On Screen

- Extreme close-up of the problem area: cracked heels, yellow teeth, thinning scalp, fungal nails, belly fat, skin tags
- May include a finger pointing at the problem, or hands pulling/stretching skin
- Speaker may appear as tiny PiP in corner, or fully absent

## Examples Observed

- amazon_cracked_heels (0:00-0:05): Macro cracked heel skin texture with foam, speaker PiP
- burner_003: Man's belly being grabbed/squeezed, elderly speaker as PiP
- burner_001: Woman's mirror selfie pointing at legs showing skin condition
- amazon_restore_hair (0:00-0:07): Close-up of scalp/hair parting showing thinning

## Visual Details

- Tight framing — body part fills 70-100% of frame
- Unflattering/raw lighting. Not beautified. The uglier, the better for scroll-stop
- May use UGC-style (phone camera, bathroom lighting) or AI-generated photorealistic
- Text overlay: hook line ("Cracked heels are not always just dry skin")
- Duration: 2-5s. Just long enough to register the problem
- Speaker PiP (if present) is very small, bottom corner

## Pipeline

- Image Prompter: 1 photorealistic close-up image of the body condition
- Be careful with Google safety filters: avoid combining body parts + sharp objects + directional movement
- No Veo needed for most (static image with text overlay in post)
- If motion desired (finger pointing, skin being touched): Veo/Seedance short clip (2-4s)
