# AI Avatar Historical

<!-- analysis-date: 2026-03-26 -->
> **Reference data**: Analyzed 2026-03-26. Source accounts: @zxaspoa. Video had no audio (HEVC, voiceover added in post). Upload date unknown — likely 2025.

AI-generated avatar narrates a historical or ancient-discovery story. The avatar alternates between full-frame talking head and a small PiP inset over full-bleed AI-generated historical imagery. Voiceover is layered in post-production, not generated by Veo.

## When to Use

- Ancient remedy / historical discovery narratives ("slave woman found a golden root")
- "Hidden knowledge suppressed by elites" conspiracy angles
- Stories involving tribes, ancient civilizations, historical figures
- Any script that frames a supplement as a rediscovered secret
- "They are trying to silence him" angles where historical imagery IS the content

## Visual Structure

```
[FULL BLEED: AI historical illustration, blurred/dreamy pastel overlay, sets time period]
    ↓ cut
[FULL FRAME: AI avatar talking head, living room, selfie angle]
    ↓ alternates
[PiP: Avatar shrinks to bottom-left inset + AI historical B-roll fills entire background]
    ↓ back to
[FULL FRAME: AI avatar talking head]
    ↓ emotional peak
[PiP: Avatar inset + vivid/painterly historical scene (dramatic moment)]
    ↓ CTA transition
[FULL FRAME: REAL mirror selfie, breaks AI aesthetic for authenticity]
    ↓
[FULL FRAME: AI avatar + red CTA arrow]
```

- Avatar occupies 100% of the frame during standard speaking scenes
- Historical imagery fills 100% of the frame during PiP scenes, avatar shrinks to a small circular inset (bottom-left)
- Two visual registers for historical B-roll: grayscale/sepia for factual scenes, vivid/painterly for emotional peaks
- Real mirror selfie at CTA deliberately breaks the AI aesthetic to add authenticity at the conversion moment
- AI disclaimer on screen throughout: "AI Generated Visuals, For Illustration Only. Not Medical Advice."
- Text overlays: white bold caps with one red accent word per line
- Red downward arrow on final CTA frame
- NO audio in the generated video. Voiceover is recorded or cloned separately and layered in post.

## Camera

- AI avatar: selfie-style, slight upward tilt, warm indoor living room
- Historical B-roll: varies by scene (wide establishing shots for locations, medium portraits for figures)
- 9:16 vertical throughout
- Smartphone aesthetic for avatar, painterly/cinematic for historical imagery

## Reference Image Groups

- **Base speaking image** (full prompt): AI avatar in living room setting, no product. Character reference with identity lock.
- **Historical B-roll images** (full prompt): Period-specific AI illustrations. Two styles:
  - Grayscale/sepia for factual scenes (factories, fields, historical settings)
  - Vivid/painterly for emotional peaks (burning at stake, golden sunset moments)
- **Opening illustration** (full prompt): Heavily blurred/washed historical scene with dreamy pastel overlay (pinks, purples, yellows). Sets the time period.
- **Real mirror selfie** (optional): Actual smartphone mirror selfie for CTA break. Can be filmed manually or generated with smartphone-in-mirror aesthetic.

## Scene Segmentation

- All Veo scenes 8 seconds (Veo 3.1 constraint)
- 22-30 words per scene
- Avatar maintains ONE consistent angle and setting throughout (no angle changes)
- Scene types alternate: full-frame speaking → PiP over historical → full-frame speaking
- Historical B-roll images are standalone scenes (full-bleed), not brief cutaways
- CTA section uses real footage or a deliberate aesthetic break

## Key Differences from Other Types

**vs. Talking Head**: Standard talking head uses real-shot B-roll cutaways that briefly replace the avatar. AI Avatar Historical uses AI-generated themed imagery that fills the ENTIRE background while the avatar shrinks to a PiP inset. The avatar alternates between being the focus (full-frame) and being the narrator (PiP over imagery).

**vs. Borrowed Authority**: Borrowed authority uses real/stock speaker footage as wallpaper behind the avatar. AI Avatar Historical uses purpose-built AI imagery that tells the actual story. The imagery IS the content, not just credibility wallpaper.

**vs. Green Screen Commentary**: Green screen places the speaker over a static or looping background. AI Avatar Historical dynamically switches between full-frame and PiP, and the background imagery changes with each narrative beat.

## Narrative Angles

Angles from source material that fit this format:

- **"Slave Woman / Zara" story**: Enslaved woman discovers a golden root, plantation wives accuse her of witchcraft. Sepia factory/field imagery for context, vivid fire scene for the accusation climax.
- **Ancient tribes**: Indigenous peoples using natural remedies for centuries. Archaeological/anthropological imagery.
- **"They are trying to silence him"**: Conspiracy angle where a discovery was suppressed by medical establishment or elites. Historical courtroom, laboratory, or institutional imagery.
- **Historical medical discoveries**: Real or fictionalized stories of ingredients found in specific historical contexts. Period-appropriate settings and clothing.

## Example

Historical supplement narrative: 10-14 scenes total. 6-8 full-frame avatar speaking scenes, 3-5 PiP scenes with historical B-roll behind avatar inset, 1 opening historical illustration (blurred/dreamy), 1 real mirror selfie for CTA break. Single avatar setting (warm living room). Historical imagery spans 2-3 time periods or locations. Voiceover recorded separately and synced in post.
