## What It Does

Wellness supplement that combats the physical and mental effects of chronic stress by targeting the root cause: elevated cortisol levels.

**Core functions:**
- **Cortisol reduction** — Lowers chronically elevated cortisol, shifts the nervous system out of "permanent overdrive"
- **Energy & mental clarity** — Fights fatigue, provides steady energy, clears mental fog, sharpens the mind
- **Symptom reversal** — Reverses visible stress/hormonal symptoms: belly weight, facial puffiness, bloating, thinning hair, loose/sagging skin

## Key Ingredient

**Rhodiola Rosea** — The core botanical. Positioned as a powerful, natural adaptogen that works biologically to balance the body. Provides stress relief and restores vitality without synthetic chemicals. Often framed as an ancient remedy with historical roots.
