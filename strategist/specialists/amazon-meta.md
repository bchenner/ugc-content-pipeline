# Amazon + Meta Specialist

Platform-specific strategy for Instagram/Facebook content driving Amazon affiliate sales. Read this when generating Content Briefs targeting the Amazon funnel via IG/FB.

---

## Platform Profile

- **Audience type**: Warm — you build followers who see your content daily and trust your avatar
- **Revenue model**: Pool-based — 20% of brand's total Amazon revenue split among creators based on clicks + views
- **Compliance**: Much more lenient than TikTok. Doctor avatars allowed. Lower ban risk.
- **Content lifespan**: Long — followers compound over time. Pages become long-term assets
- **AI label**: NOT required on Meta platforms. No AI-flagging penalties.

## Amazon Affiliate Funnel

```
Video on IG/FB → CTA "comment HEALTH" → ManyChat auto-DM → Amazon affiliate link → Purchase
```

### Step by step:
1. Post video with clear CTA telling viewers to comment a keyword (e.g., "HEALTH", "LINK", "GLOW")
2. ManyChat detects the keyword comment and automatically sends a DM
3. The DM provides immediate value (recipe, health tip, hack) + a button with your Amazon affiliate link
4. Viewer clicks → lands on Amazon product page → purchases
5. Sale tracked via your Amazon Associates short link

### Why this works:
- Viewer has seen your avatar multiple times → trusts the recommendation
- DM feels personal and private → higher conversion than a public link
- ManyChat handles everything automatically — no manual DM replies needed
- You get paid even for "indirect" sales (viewer watches but searches Amazon manually later)

## Revenue Model: The 20% Pool

Unlike TikTok Shop (direct affiliate), the Amazon strategy uses a shared pool:

1. **Brand takes 20% of total Amazon revenue** (includes organic search, subscriptions, repeat buyers) → puts it in a creator bonus pool
2. **Your share** is based on two metrics:
   - **Amazon link clicks** (tracked via your Associates link)
   - **Total US views** across all platforms (IG, FB, TikTok)
3. **Payout**: Monthly. Submit screenshots of Associates dashboard (clicks) + social analytics (views, US audience %) to the course portal. PayPal or bank transfer.

**Key advantage**: You get paid for brand awareness even if viewers don't click your link directly.

## Account Setup

### Amazon Associates
1. Go to `affiliate-program.amazon.com`, sign up for US account
2. Input personal info, paste Instagram link under "website or mobile apps"
3. Declare yourself as a UGC creator
4. Go to the specific product listing → click "Get Link" → copy your unique short link

### Growi Registration
1. Click the provided Growi invite link
2. Enter full name and real email, submit verification code
3. Submit links to your active TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook pages
4. For Facebook: use the full profile URL (`facebook.com/profile.php?id=...`), not username

### Instagram Account
1. Create new Instagram account
2. Switch to **Creator Account** (NOT Business account)
3. Set profile: AI avatar as photo, health/wellness niche bio, consistent name

### Facebook Page
1. Use a parent Facebook profile to create a dedicated **Facebook Page**
2. Category: "Health and Wellness"
3. Matching name, bio, and avatar as Instagram

## Content Strategy

### The 2:1 → 1:1 Ratio

| Follower Count | Educational : Product | Why |
|---------------|----------------------|-----|
| Under 50k | **2:1** (2 growth, 1 sales) | Build trust, grow audience, earn algorithm favor |
| Over 50k | **1:1** (equal mix) | Audience trusts you — monetize harder |

### Educational (Follower Growth) Videos
- **Goal**: Grow page, build trust, gain algorithm momentum
- **Content**: Tips, recipes, motivation, health facts, lifestyle advice
- **Length**: 30-45 seconds (max 90)
- **CTA**: "Follow for more" or "Comment HEALTH" (soft CTA → still triggers ManyChat)
- **Goes viral easier** because it provides free value with no hard sell

### Product Promotion (Sales) Videos
- **Goal**: Drive conversions
- **Structure**: Specific pain point → advice → position product as solution → hard CTA
- **Length**: Up to 2 minutes
- **CTA**: "Comment LINK to get it" → ManyChat sends Amazon affiliate link
- **Fewer views** but massively higher conversion rate
- Script does NOT need to sell as hard as TikTok — audience already trusts you

## ManyChat Setup

1. Link Instagram to ManyChat
2. Set up automation per post: when user comments keyword → auto-send DM
3. DM structure:
   - Immediate value (recipe, health tip, or quick hack)
   - Then: "Here's what I've been using →" + button with Amazon affiliate link
   - NOT just a bare link — value first, link second
4. Keywords per video: "HEALTH", "LINK", "ROUTINE", "GLOW", etc.

## Comment Management

### Comment Hiding
- Go to Instagram Settings → Hidden Words
- Input keywords to auto-hide (spam, negative, competitor mentions)
- Also works on Facebook
- Check comment sections after videos go viral — add new blocked keywords as needed

### Burner Accounts
- Use a separate account (or your main page) to train the algorithm
- Search competitor brand keywords (Rosabella, Moringa, Beetroot, Serene Herbs)
- Watch their reels in full, engage, save, comment
- Use daily — watch time is critical for algorithm training
- Don't skip reels too fast

## Posting Strategy

- **Frequency**: 3 videos/day per account (sweet spot). 1/day absolute minimum.
- **Timing**: Doesn't matter — just wait 2-3 hours between posts
- **Cross-posting**: Same video file → Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Threads, YouTube Shorts
- **Scheduling**: Native IG/TikTok scheduling is fine — no negative impact on views
- **80-90% of revenue comes from Instagram and Facebook** — these are the priority platforms

## Daily Workflow (Batching)

Target: 9-12 videos/day across all accounts. Three rigid phases — never mix them:

**Phase 1: Research & Ideas**
- Browse TikTok and Instagram for viral content
- Collect video ideas, hooks, background footage for the entire day
- Use burner account to stay on top of trends

**Phase 2: Scripting**
- Write ALL scripts back-to-back (e.g., all 12)
- Use 80/20 rule: find viral scripts, keep 80% structure, change 20%
- Do NOT check messages or switch tasks during this phase

**Phase 3: Generate & Edit**
- Work video by video — animate, edit, export ONE complete video before starting the next
- Do NOT generate multiple videos simultaneously (mixing up assets)
- Avatar animation → layer over B-roll → add CTA → export

## Top 10 Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Switching avatars** — destroys trust, forces restart
2. **Unrealistic avatars** — blurry/robotic characters make people scroll
3. **Bad account branding** — unclear bio, inconsistent profile, no niche
4. **Blurry background videos** — download high-quality footage only
5. **No hook** — first 3 seconds must grab attention or video fails
6. **Script too long** — growth: 30-45s max 90s, sales: max 2 min
7. **Weak/no CTA** — great video without "comment HEALTH" wastes views
8. **Only posting recipes** — diversify backgrounds (lifestyle, health facts, motivation)
9. **Low posting volume** — posting every few days won't trigger algorithm
10. **Never asking for feedback** — spamming bad videos without coaching = repeating mistakes
