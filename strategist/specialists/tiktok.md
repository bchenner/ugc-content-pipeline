# TikTok Specialist

Platform-specific strategy for TikTok Shop content. Read this when generating Content Briefs targeting TikTok.

---

## Platform Profile

- **Audience type**: Cold — you reach strangers via For You page, not followers
- **Revenue model**: Direct affiliate — you only earn when someone clicks YOUR video's Shop button and buys
- **Compliance**: Strictest of all platforms. Frequent ban waves, medical claim flags, account suspensions
- **Content lifespan**: Short — viral spike then dies. No compounding trust like IG/FB
- **AI label**: Required for AI-generated content (see AI Label Trick below)

---

## TikTok Shop Funnel

```
Video on FYP → Shop button on video → Direct purchase → Commission
```

- Sale must happen through YOUR video's shop link
- No ManyChat, no comment keywords, no DM funnels
- Script must do ALL the selling — viewer is a stranger with zero trust
- 30-90 second videos are the sweet spot

---

## Compliance Rules (Current as of March 2026)

| Element | Risk Level | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| Doctor avatar | BANNED | No longer allowed on TikTok Shop |
| Direct health claims | HIGH | Instant flag, potential account suspension |
| Before/after body transformation | HIGH | "After" photos get flagged in ban waves |
| Lifestyle transformation (activities) | LOW | Current winning format — activities not body parts |
| Storytelling / history | LOW | Safest format, narrative framing |
| Podcast-style interviews | LOW | Personal experience, not medical claims |
| Supplement mention by name | MEDIUM | Risky if combined with health claims |
| "I'm not a doctor" disclaimer | DOES NOT HELP | Platform flags content regardless |

**Ban waves**: TikTok periodically sweeps accounts with mass violations. Can take down accounts and wipe income instantly. Volume of accounts matters — don't put all eggs in one basket.

**⚠️ March 20 update**: Violations and ban waves have drastically decreased as of March 2026. Test riskier formats again — transformations, visual symptoms, before/after content. Still exercise caution but the window is more open than before. <!-- date: 2026-03 -->

### AI Label Trick

For AI-generated content on TikTok: add a 0.01-second flash of a dark screen or AI-generated face at the very end of the video. This triggers the platform's "AI generated" label automatically, which helps avoid content violation flags.

- Works on VMOS and CapCut
- Nearly invisible to the viewer (0.01s)
- Required for all AI avatar content on TikTok Shop

## Content Formats (Priority Order)

1. **Lifestyle Transformation Hook + Podcast Body** (Pattern T) — Current winning concept. Hook shows before/after of daily ACTIVITY (not body part) → cuts to podcast body for pitch + CTA
2. **Storytelling** (Pattern P) — Historical/cultural narratives tied to health discovery. Safest format
3. **Podcast Storytelling** (Pattern Q) — AI character delivers personal UGC-style story
4. **Direct Transformation** — Before/after body progression. HIGH RISK — only if account is NOT getting flagged

**Key difference from IG/FB**: Script must sell hard because viewer has zero prior trust. Every second counts.

---

## Scripting for TikTok

- **Hook in first 3 seconds** — must stop the scroll immediately
- **Shocking statement or belief-breaking opener** — "The first medicine wasn't invented in America" / "Whatever you do, don't move in with your wife"
- **Open loops** — introduce a shocking concept after the hook, refuse to reveal until the end
- **Short and punchy** — 30-45s for growth content, max 90s for sales
- **80/20 rule** — Find viral scripts, keep 80% of structure, change 20% for your product/angle
- **Hard CTA** — Direct to shop button. No soft CTAs on TikTok

---

## Visual Requirements

- **Visual Symptom Pyramid**: Symptom must be VISIBLE from second 1 — crutches, sitting down mid-task, rubbing legs, struggling to walk
- **Before scene**: Daily activity struggle, not body-part close-up
- **After scene**: Same activity done effortlessly, warm/positive lighting
- **Never**: Direct body-part transformation (triggers bans)
- **Avatar**: Must look like a credible health authority (holistic grandmother, wellness coach — NOT doctor)

---

## Platform-Specific Notes

- TikTok pushes content to cold audiences — algorithm discovery is king
- Video metadata doesn't need to differ from other platforms — same file works
- Wait 2-3 hours between posts
- 3 videos/day is the growth sweet spot, 1/day is bare minimum
- Post to TikTok, Threads, and YouTube Shorts simultaneously
- Use burner accounts to train algorithm and discover trending formats
