| Field | Detail |

| Dosage | 2 capsules daily |

| Price point | ~$20 |
| Key ingredient | Rhodiola Rosea (adaptogen, "golden root") |
| USP | 100% pure, no fillers, non-GMO, third party tested, single ingredient |

### What It Does

Adaptogen supplement that helps the body manage stress and restore balance. Supports:
- Sleep quality
- Steady energy (not stimulant energy)
- Mental clarity / brain fog reduction
- Mood balance
- Stress recovery

### How to Talk About It in Scripts

**DO say:**
- "Helps the body manage stress naturally"
- "An adaptogen that supports your body's stress response"
- "My patients tell me their sleep came back first, then the energy"
- "It helps the body remember how to recover"
- "Ancient root used for centuries"
- "Two capsules in the morning, that's it"

**DO NOT say:**
- "Cortisol" (banned word — use "stress" instead)
- "Burns fat" / "weight loss" / "belly fat" (banned)
- "Cures" / "treats" / "prevents" (medical claim)
- "Guaranteed results" / "works in X days" (without disclaimer)
- "Better than prescription medication"
- "Body transformation" / "look 10 years younger"

### Approved Emotional Angles

| Angle | What It Means | Script Approach |
|---|---|---|
| Menopause | Hot flashes, mood swings, sleep loss, relationship strain | Wife hero journey, practitioner recounting patient story |
| Looking old / unattractive | Husband left, lost glow, aging shame | Hero daughter helps mom glow up |
| Loss of female energy | Burnt out mom, can't cope, running on empty | Woman herself, burnout confession |
| Stress | Chronic stress destroying relationships, health, joy | Any avatar, symptom ID |
| Loss of sexual drive | Intimacy breakdown, low desire | Daughter or woman herself |

### Banned Angles

- **Cortisol** — do not use this word in any script
- **Weight loss** — no belly fat, no body transformation, no diet framing
- **Body transformation claims** — no before/after body changes
